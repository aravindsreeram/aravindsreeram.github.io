About Me

Hi, I'm Aravind! I'm a first year medical student interested in clinical outcomes research. I've had the privilege of working in several clinical and translational labs in the past which have given me a wide skillset that I hope to use in a future career as a surgeon-scientist. My current interests include oncology, neuroscience, and patient well-being.
